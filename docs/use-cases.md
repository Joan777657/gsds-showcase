# Use Cases

## Public-Facing Use Case Framing

This document describes the kinds of future-facing application contexts that **GSDS ESG Diagnostic Engine** is intended to support at a conceptual level.

It is maintained as part of the public showcase materials of **Golden Silver Sustainability Strategies Co., Ltd. (GSDS)** and is limited to non-sensitive, reviewer-friendly framing.

---

## Purpose of This Page

This page is intended to help external readers understand where GSDS sees structured ESG capability becoming useful in practice.

It does not present product logic, scoring methodology, internal rule structures, implementation workflows, or client delivery templates.

---

## Illustrative Public Use Cases

Potential future use cases may include:

- SME ESG readiness support
- governance and planning support for service-oriented organizations
- KPI discussion and implementation planning support
- ecosystem interpretation and stakeholder communication support
- structured ESG productization pathways

These examples are presented as directional use cases only. They should not be interpreted as a disclosure of underlying commercial methodology or delivery architecture.

---

## What These Use Cases Demonstrate

Taken together, these use cases demonstrate that GSDS views ESG capability as something that can be:

- structured for organizational use
- translated into practical planning language
- aligned with governance and accountability needs
- developed into future tools, services, or platform layers

This reinforces the public positioning of GSDS ESG Diagnostic Engine as a capability framework and concept-layer product direction.

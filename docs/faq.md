# FAQ

## Public Showcase FAQ

This FAQ supports reviewer-friendly interpretation of the public repository for **GSDS ESG Diagnostic Engine**, maintained by **Golden Silver Sustainability Strategies Co., Ltd. (GSDS)**.

---

## What is this repository for?

This repository exists to present GSDS's public-facing product direction, ESG capability framing, and systems-oriented design approach in a conservative, brand-safe format.

It is intended for external review, partner discussion, and high-level capability understanding.

---

## Is this the full GSDS diagnostic system?

No.

This repository is a **public showcase layer** only. It is not a release of GSDS's internal commercial diagnostic system, internal advisory framework, or client-delivery environment.

---

## Does this repository include scoring models or diagnostic logic?

No.

This repository does not disclose proprietary scoring models, scoring details, diagnostic logic, internal rule libraries, or implementation details that could reconstruct the commercial mother framework.

---

## Does this repository include client materials?

No.

This repository does not include client delivery templates, customer-specific data, restricted advisory materials, or confidential workflow structures.

---

## What should reviewers understand from this repository?

Reviewers should understand that GSDS has:

- a real method direction
- structured product thinking
- a coherent ESG systems perspective
- a public-facing capability framework that may support future development

The repository is designed to communicate direction and discipline, not disclose protected methodology.

---

## Who maintains GSDS ESG Diagnostic Engine?

**GSDS ESG Diagnostic Engine** is maintained by **Golden Silver Sustainability Strategies Co., Ltd. (GSDS)**.

Any reference to GSDS in this repository should be understood as referring to that company.

---

## Can this repository be used to reproduce GSDS's internal framework?

No.

The repository is intentionally scoped to prevent disclosure of internal structures, proprietary logic, and commercially sensitive assets. It is designed for public understanding, not operational replication.

---

## Where should readers look for brand and usage context?

Readers should refer to `README.md` and `BRAND_NOTICE.md` for the repository's public positioning, brand context, and usage boundary.
